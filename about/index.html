TL;DR: I am a salesforce Solution Engineer🏄‍♂️ pro-code enthusiast 🔭 self-taught wannabe coder 🧙🏼‍♂️ (JavaScript, Java). Planet lover 🌎🌿

My salesforce journey started accidentally in the autum 2017, during my consulting internship at Deloitte CE South Offices in Tirana, Albania 🇦🇱. At the time my first project was a highly complex German Auto manufacturing project. I had not much of an idea what sfdc really was, but then I started doing badges in Trailhead and solving challanges on the side. I really loved it and I consider myself so lucky to work in such an exciting industry. Proving solutions and helping businesses have a greater ROI on their digitalization projects it’s the best thing I could have asked for my career.

In 2018, I relocated back to Germany 🇩🇪, where I also studied during my Masters degree. The new role was that of the Technical Consultant at adesso in Cologne. I did a lot of trainings including FSL, Requirement Engineering and Agile Framework. As a TC, I focused more on the complex projects (Financial Services & B2B Commerce CoudCraze) to deliver features based on the client’s requirements or build POCs.

Since 2020, I have been working at salesfive 💙, as a Solution Engineer, architecting mainly in enterprise projects based on the Sales, Service & FSL Cloud. During this time, in addition to the common clouds platforms, I gained a lot of expertise in B2B Commerce Cloud for Lightning, APEX developement, ALM, Heroku etc.

I am a big fan of flows but I also love pro-code and LWCs. Deciding in my project when to use declarative vs a programmatic tools is excting because of the importance of the decision but also because it is a great deal when it comes to the future maintability and performance of the system.

Lately, I have been working a lot on CI/CD pipelines to bring the latest technology (think about: scratch orgs, orchestrators, unlocked packages) in my team, so it’s easier and faster to deploy features in sandboxes and hotfixes in production 🌀. #ALMisExciting

Since March 2022, I am also the Group Leader of Trailblazer Architects in Düsseldorf, where we present architecture and developer topics every 1-2 months, especially integration and identity and access management topics. Join the group if you also in the North-West Westfalia region 🚀.
