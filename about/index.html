I started accidentally working on the salesforce platform in 2017, during my first consulting internship at Deloitte CE in Tirana, Albania🇦🇱. In 2018, I relocated back to Germany🇩🇪, where I also studied during my Masters degree. The new role was that of the technical consutant at adesso in Cologne.

I love flows but I am also a big fan of pro-code and LWCs.

Lately, I have been working a lot on CI/CD pipelines to bring the latest technology (think about: scratch orgs, orchestrators, unlocked packages) in my team, so it’s easier and faster to deploy features in sandboxes and hotfixes in production🌀.

Since March 2022, I am also the Group Leader of Trailblazer Architects in Düsseldorf, where we present architecture and developer topics every 1-2 months.
