🎉 Last Architect Trailblazer Meeting for year 2022 (Pictures)

Enea Gjoka
2022-12-22
community, salesforce
⏱ 1 min. read

Intro

On Thursday, December 15th, at 18:30 (CET) we hosted our last Salesforce Architect Group in-person Meeting in Düsseldorf, Germany for the year 2022.

🙌🏼 This last half year of 2022 we have 3 awesome meetings:

  • Summer 2022 Meet & Greet (August)
  • PMD Source Code Analysis, PR Reviews and Dreamforce Stickers (November)
  • Open-Source Packages any Project can rock 🎸🤘🏼 (December)

Next Year plans

We aim to make the Düsseldorf Architect Group one of the most active groups in the whole DACH region. I am working in having speakers for new interesting topics, and hopefully inviting over a couple of MVPs too.
